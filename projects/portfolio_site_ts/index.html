About Me

Although I've been around computers for many years, it wasn't until my time as a researcher in grad school did I cam across coding and computer programming. As a researcher, I had to perform various tasks that could only be done through a computer, so I taught myself to code functional programs, first with Bash scripts, then with Python.

It wasn't until I've built my very first website from scratch, however, did I find something special. Although it had been a very simple website, one that documented my time as a programmer, building it from scratch had been a fun and refreshing experience, one I wish to indulge in. And I've been coding away ever since, applying what I learned and starting to build websites.

So check out some of my creative projects below whether it be on your mobile device or on your desktop!
