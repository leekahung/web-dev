About Me

Although I've been around computers for many years, it wasn't until my time as a researcher in grad school did I came across the world of coding. It wasn't until I've built my very first website from scratch in 2021, however, did I find something special in coding. Building it from scratch had been a fun and refreshing experience for me at the time, one I wish to further indulge in. And I've been coding away on websites ever since, applying what I learned and building them using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
